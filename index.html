ATTENDING AN EVENT WITH
A SafeTix MOBILE TICKET

SafeTix by Ticketmaster is today’s most secure mobile ticket technology, featuring an encrypted barcode that refreshes every 15 seconds, to protect your tickets from being screenshot or photocopied. With this ticket delivery method, your mobile ticket will be displayed on your phone for entry into your event. Tickets will NOT be emailed or available to print.

Please note: Not all tickets are eligible for mobile delivery and ticket transfer. I fan event is not eligible, you will be presented with other delivery options.

Images on this page may contain illustrations referencing customer, event or venue names not applicable to your purchase. Additionally, illustrations may vary slight depending on your mobile device or the web version in use.

HOW TO ACCESS YOUR MOBILE TICKET
Live Nation Clients:
Login to AccountManager to manage your tickets. Each venue at Live Nation has a designated AccountManager site. AccountManager is mobile-optimized and you can access all features from either your mobile device or desktop computer.  Once you receive a site link from your venue sales representative, you can Sign In using your e-mail address and password. If this is your first time logging in, select ‘Forgot Password?’ Enter the email address associated with your premium seats purchase and a link will be sent to you in order to set a personalized password for future use. You may also visit here to reset your password at any time. (AccountManager is a separate system from your Livenation.com or Ticketmaster.com account, should you have one. Your tickets are not currently manageable with the Live Nation app.)

Guests Accepting Tickets:
If you are the recipient of a ticket transfer, please scroll down to ‘Receiving A Ticket Transfer.’
Log into AccountManager
If you are new to AccountManager and your account does not share an email address with an existing Ticketmaster.com / LiveNation.com account, please go to ‘New to Ticketmaster? Sign Up’ If you already have a Ticketmaster.com / Livenation.com account with the same email address, your password for that account will also work for AccountManager. You may be prompted to reset your password when you first login. Select Sign In.    Select Forgot Password? at any time if you’ve forgotten your password. Guests Accepting A Ticket Transfer: If you are the recipient of a ticket transfer, please review ‘Receiving A Ticket Transfer.’ We recommend accepting a ticket transfer on your mobile device.
VIEWING & USING YOUR TICKETS

Login to AccountManager on your mobile device.

Select the event for the tickets you’d like to view.

Select View Barcode to view your mobile ticket.

iPhone Users: Select ‘Add to Apple Wallet’ on the ticket.

After adding the ticket(s) to your Apple Wallet, the barcode will no longer be visible. Tap and Go!  Simply tap your phone to the ticket scanner at the venue for entry.

AndroidUsers:  Select ‘Google Pay Save to phone’ on the ticket.

After adding the ticket(s) to Google Pay, the mobile ticket will display a bar code framed by a dynamically colored hologram. Simply show the barcode on your mobile ticket to the ticket scanner at the venue for entry.

TRANSFERRING TICKETS
Go to View All Tickets and find the event you’d like to manage.  Select Transfer.
In the event, select the specific ticket or all tickets you’d like to transfer. Select Next.
Enter the first name, last name, email address or Mobile Number for your intended recipient. Select Transfer.
Confirm your intended recipient for the transfer. Select Transfer.  Note: Recipient information can be saved for future transfers.
A success message will display for you after the transfer has been initiated. The transfer is now pending acceptance by the recipient.
A confirmation email for the ticket transfer initiation will be sent to you and the recipient.
A ‘Waiting for Recipient to Claim’ status will display on any ticket while transfer acceptance is pending.
After the recipient has accepted the ticket(s), a ‘Claimed’ status will display and the ticket(s) will move out of the original account and into the recipient’s account.
ACCEPTING A TICKET TRANSFER

As the intended recipient of a ticket transfer, you will receive a ‘Your Tickets From…’ email asking you to accept the tickets. Select Accept Tickets. You may also decline the transfer.

Alternatively, you may receive an SMS message if the ticket transfer was sent to your mobile phone number rather than your email address. Click on the link to proceed.

If you are new to AccountManager and your account does not share an email address with an existing Ticketmaster.com / LiveNation.com account, please go to ‘New to Ticketmaster? Sign Up’

If you already have a Ticketmaster.com / Livenation.com account with the same email address, your password for that account will also work for AccountManager. You may be prompted to reset your password when you first login. Select Sign In.

Select Forgot Password? at any time if you’ve forgotten your password.

After logging in to AccountManager, you will be prompted to Agree with out Terms & Conditions (Ticketmaster andLive Nation Premium Seat Sales).

On the ticket(s) being transferred to you, select Accept to claim the tickets (or Not Now if you’d like to revisit later).

Once the tickets have been accepted, the tickets are now yours and in your account to manage moving forward.

An email confirming the acceptance of the ticket transfer will be sent to you as well as the sender.

SelectView Barcode to view your mobile ticket.  

iPhone Users:  Select ‘Add to Apple Wallet’ on the ticket.

After adding the ticket(s) to your Apple Wallet, the barcode will no longer be visible. Tap and Go!  Simply tap your phone to the ticket scanner at the venue for entry.

Android Users: Select ‘Google Pay Save to phone’ on the ticket.

After adding the ticket(s) to Google Pay, the mobile ticket will display a barcode framed by a dynamically colored hologram. Simply show the barcode on your mobile ticket to the ticket scanner at the venue for entry.

CANCELLING A TICKET TRANSFER

If you wish to cancel a ticket transfer you’ve initiated, find the ticket(s) and select Cancel Transfer.  Note: The ability to cancel is no longer available once a transfer is claimed.

  Select Cancel.

A success message will appear once the ticket transfer is cancelled and the tickets will remain in your account.

An email confirming the cancellation of the ticket transfer will be sent to you as well as the intended recipient.

RESELLING TICKETS
*Available only for reserved seats & lawn tickets

Locate the ticket(s)* for the event you would like to resell. Select Sell.

*Available for only reserved seats & lawn tickets

Select each ticket you would like to resell. Select Next.

If available, AccountManager will display Price Guidance to help ensure you are listing tickets for market value. You may adjust your listing price which will then adjust the amount you will ultimately be paid.
Select your Payout Method (Check by Mail or Direct Deposit) and Add Payee Info. After filling in all necessary information, select Submit Listing. Note: You may adjust the price after the listing goes public.
MANAGE TICKETS VIA THE LIVE NATION APP

Open or download the Live Nation (LN) App on Apple or Android devices.

Go to My Tickets at the bottom of the screen view to login to the LN App.

Sign In to the LN App with your email address and password previously used to purchase tickets through Livenation.com or Ticketmaster.com.

After logging into the LN App, you will see tickets to any upcoming LN events you’ve purchased on either LN.com or Ticketmaster.com.

At the top of your screen, select the down arrow where you see the “t” Ticketmaster logo and your email address. Select Sign Into LiveNation Premium Seats.

On the Get Your Live Nation Premium Seats Tickets Sign In page, please sign in with your AccountManager email address and password.
‍
Note: LN App login may or may not be the same as your AccountManager login. It is not a requirement for them to match.

After you have logged in with your AccountManager credentials, you will see your name at the top of the screen with two logos:“LN”/ “t.” This is your indication that you now have access to manage all of your Live Nation event tickets, as you are now logged into both AccountManager and your LN / Ticketmaster accounts.

Note: If you have access to multiple AccountManager sites across LN venues, all of your tickets will be visible. If you login to AccountManager directly (not through the LN App), only the premium seat tickets for that venue will be visible.
